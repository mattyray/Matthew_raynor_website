My Journey

Software Engineer. Photographer. Writer.

After a diving accident left me paralyzed, I spent 30 months in long-term care. I turned a tiny meditation room into my classroom — studying software engineering, learning Spanish, and diving deep into Buddhism, Taoism, and breathwork to overcome what my body couldn't do.

Now I build AI-powered platforms, create aerial photography exhibited across the Hamptons, and write about resilience, spirituality, and transformation. My disability isn't my limitation — it's the reason I found my purpose.
